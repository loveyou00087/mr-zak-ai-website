What is Artificial Intelligence (AI)?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the simulation of human intelligence in machines. These machines are programmed to think like humans and mimic their actions. In simple terms, AI is a technology that enables computers and machines to perform tasks that usually require human intelligence.

Learn More

What is Artificial Intelligence?

AI is a branch of computer science that focuses on creating systems that can perform tasks that would normally require human intelligence. These tasks include learning, reasoning, problem-solving, understanding language, and perception.

Example-Based Explanation

Think of AI like a very smart assistant that learns from experience. For example:

  • Voice Assistants: Siri or Alexa understand your voice commands and respond.
  • Recommendation Systems: Netflix suggests movies you might like based on what you've watched before.
  • Self-Driving Cars: They use AI to recognize roads, signs, and obstacles to drive safely.

Disadvantages of AI

Job Risks

AI automation may replace certain jobs, especially repetitive tasks in manufacturing, data entry, and customer service.

Over-dependency

Over-reliance on AI systems may reduce human skills and decision-making abilities.

Privacy Issues

AI systems often require large amounts of data, raising concerns about data privacy and security.
